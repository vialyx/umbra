Welcome to Umbra

Automatic Mac locking based on device proximity

Umbra automatically locks your Mac when you walk away with your iPhone or Apple Watch, providing seamless security for your workspace.

Monitor multiple devices simultaneously
Customizable distance and delay settings
Runs silently in the background
Menu bar controls for easy access

System Requirements:

  • macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later
  • Bluetooth 4.0 or later
  • iPhone or Apple Watch

This installer will install Umbra in your Applications folder and set it up to launch automatically on login.
