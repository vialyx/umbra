Getting Started with Umbra

After installation, Umbra will start automatically. Look for the shield icon in your menu bar.

⚠️ Important Permissions Required

Umbra needs the following permissions to work properly:

  • Bluetooth: Automatically requested when scanning
  • Accessibility: Required to lock your screen
  • Notifications: Optional, for lock alerts

You'll be prompted to grant these permissions when you first use Umbra.

Quick Setup

  1. Click the Umbra icon in your menu bar
  2. Select Settings...
  3. Go to the Devices tab
  4. Click Scan for Devices
  5. Make sure your iPhone/Apple Watch is nearby and Bluetooth is on
  6. Click the + button next to your device to start monitoring

Configuration Tips

Distance Threshold: Start with -70 dBm (default). This is approximately 3-5 meters. Lower values = shorter distance.

Lock Delay: We recommend 5 seconds to prevent accidental locks from brief signal drops.

Multiple Devices: You can monitor multiple devices. Your Mac will lock when ALL monitored devices are out of range.

Testing

Before relying on Umbra:

  1. Set up your device(s) for monitoring
  2. Go to Advanced tab
  3. Click Test Lock Screen to verify locking works
  4. Walk away with your device to test the distance threshold

Troubleshooting

Bluetooth is off: Enable Bluetooth in System Settings → Bluetooth

Can't see my device: Make sure your device is unlocked and Bluetooth is enabled on it

Screen won't lock: Check Accessibility permissions in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility

Uninstallation

To completely remove Umbra:

  1. Quit Umbra from the menu bar
  2. Delete /Applications/Umbra.app
  3. Delete ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.umbra.app.plist
  4. Run: launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.umbra.app.plist
